भिवृद्धिबीजगर्भसमुद्भवैनोनिबर्हणद्वारा श्रीपरमेश्वरप्रीत्यर्थ जातकर्म करिष्ये. He

pose, in honour of Agni, Indra, Prajâpati, the Vis'vedevas, and
Brahma. He shall then mix a little honey and clarified butter
together,' in unequal proportions, and put up the compound on a
fat piece of stone, and shall rub a bit of gold on it till some
portion of it shall have been mixed with honey and clarified butter
and with the same bit of gold he shall take the mixed honey and
put it in the baby's month, with this Mantra : ॐ प्र ते ददामि मधुनो
घृतस्य वेदं सवित्रा प्रसूतं मघोनाम् । आयुष्मान्गुप्तो देवताभिः शतं जीव शरदो लॉके
अस्मिन् । He shall then wash clean the bit of gold and putting it
on the right ear of the baby, he shall bring his own mouth close to
the baby’s and shall say : ॐ मेध ते देवः सविता मेधां देवी सरस्वती । मेधा ते
अश्विनौ देवावाधत्तां पुष्कर स्त्रजी | Putting again the piece of gold on the
left ear of the baby he shall repeat the same verse. He shall then
lightly touch with the span of his right hand both the shoulders
of the baby at the same moment, and repeat thus : अश्मा भव परशुर्भव
हिरण्यमस्तृतं भव | वेदो वै पुत्रनामासि स जीव शरदः शतम् । ॐ इन्द्र श्रेष्ठानि द्रवि-
णानि धेहि चित्तिं दक्षस्य सुभगत्वमस्मे । पोषं रयीणामरिष्टिं तनूनां स्वाझानं वाचः सुदि
नत्वमह्नाम् । ॐ अस्मे प्रयन्धि मघवन्नृजीषिन्निन्द्र रायो विश्ववारस्य भूरेः । अस्मे शतं
शरदो जीवसे धा अस्मे वीराच्छश्वत इन्द्र शिपिन ॥ Then, to secure well-being
and long life for the new born, he shall say : अङ्गादगारसंभवसि हृदया-
दधि जायसे । आत्मा वै पुत्रनामासि स जीव शरदः शतम् | And with this he
shall thrice smell the head of the baby. And having returned to
the sacrifice, he shall complete it. Thon with cold water, he shall
wash the right breast of the mother, and shall make her suckle the
babe with this Matra : ॐ इमां कुमारो जरां धयतु दीर्घमायुः प्रजीवसे । अस्ने
स्तनौ प्रयुञ्जाना आयुर्वच यशो बलम् । He shall then give cows, lands,
tila corn, and gold to Brahmanas as presents".
